Loans and process

Loans available for education, home purchase, home repair, family functions, medical emergencies, business needs, and grain purchase. Special “Kanya Education Loan” for girls. Micro-loans from ₹6,000 available.

Loan process

  • Application: Get a loan form from the office, fill it, attach required documents, and submit.
  • Amount: ₹6,000 to ₹20,00,000.
  • Security: Up to ₹20,000 no guarantor; ₹20,000–₹40,000 one guarantor; above ₹40,000 two guarantors (guarantors must be society members).
  • Interest: Reducing interest policy; lower interest rates.
  • Repayment: ECS, QR code, cheque, or online bank transfer.

Impact

  • Housing: 20 members built homes.
  • Two-wheelers: 120 members purchased vehicles.
  • Cars: 30 members purchased cars.
  • Livelihood: Many improved homes and businesses.
