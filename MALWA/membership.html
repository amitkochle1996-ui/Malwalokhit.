Documents required

  • Forms: Membership form
  • Photographs: 2 of applicant, 1 of nominee/heir
  • ID proofs: PAN copy, Aadhaar copy
  • Address proof: Electricity bill copy (if own house)
  • Bank proof: Front page copy of bank passbook
  • For tenants: Copy of rent agreement
